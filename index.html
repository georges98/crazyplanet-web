Crazy Planet is an Action game that let you explore different planets. The player objective is to survive in a chaotic planet, where the user might face different obstacles like houses, trees and even METEORS!! The Player can shoot rockets to eliminate Obstacles and earn points. But be aware that each rocket failing to hit a target will always be hovering over the planet and might even hit the player himself !!
